Jessica - a fifteen year old sophomore at a high school where everyone looks the same and follows the crowd. am not quite sure where exactly i fit in. music keeps me grounded; so does tyler. quiet at times, introspective, emotional, a thinker. other times silly, weird, and random. moody as hell. hates change and people who are fake. dependent on people, is scared of the responsibility that comes with growing up. against smoking, drugs, war, organized religion, and many other things. wants to design cd covers for bands when i grow up, or something else related to graphic design. probably the only person in the world who never watches t.v.
