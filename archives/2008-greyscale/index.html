I'm a senior at Virginia Tech in Blacksburg, VA, pursuing a BFA in Visual Communications Design and attempting to snag a career in print and/or web design. I'm shockingly short and need a foot stool to reach the top shelves in my kitchen. While I'm partial to sans-serifs, I do have a soft spot for italic serifs. Music is what gets me through the day and what fuels my work.

  • a spiral-bound lined notebook
  • a red mini daily planner
  • a video iPod with charger and headphones
  • a black iPhone with charger and headphones
  • a pair of teal sunglasses
  • a PowerBook power cord
  • various keys on a Virginia Tech lanyard
